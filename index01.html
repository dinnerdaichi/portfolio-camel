Admissions

Application Flow

  • STEP

    1

    Participate in Open Campus (open day) and Information session

    Participate in our online/ offline events.
    For more details, please check our website or social media

  • STEP

    2

    Take the Japanese test

    Take either the EJU or JLPT test (submission of certificate is not compulsory)

  • STEP

    3

    Check the dates and the examination categories

    Make sure to check the guideline (Japanese only) for details regarding the examinations

  • STEP

    4

    Prepare necessary documents

    Application forms, certificates and other documents

  • STEP

    5

    Register for the Internet Application and submit documents

    Every examination needs to be applied online first. Register an account then apply for the examination
